For the state of the Montana the laws of mimum wage is: Businesses with gross annual sales of more than $110,000 Basic Minimum Rate (per hour): $10.55 Premium Pay After Designated Hours The overtime premium rate is one and one-half times the employee's regular rate, unless otherwise specified. : Weekly - 40 Businesses not covered by the Fair Labor Standards Act with gross annual sales of $110,000 or less Basic Minimum Rate (per hour): $4.00 Premium Pay After Designated Hours The overtime premium rate is one and one-half times the employee's regular rate, unless otherwise specified. : Weekly - 40 A business not covered by the federal Fair Labor Standards Act whose gross annual sales are $110,000 or less may pay $4.00 per hour. However, if an individual employee is producing or moving goods between states or otherwise covered by the federal Fair Labor Standards Act, that employee must be paid the greater of either the federal minimum wage or Montana's minimum wage. The minimum wage is adjusted annually based on a set formula.
