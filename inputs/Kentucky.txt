For the state of the Kentucky the laws of mimum wage is: Basic Minimum Rate (per hour): $7.25 Premium Pay After Designated Hours The overtime premium rate is one and one-half times the employee's regular rate, unless otherwise specified. : Weekly - 40, 7th day The 7th day overtime law, which is separate from the minimum wage law, requires employers who permit covered employees to work seven days in any one workweek to pay the employee at a rate of time and one-half for hours worked on the seventh day when employees work all seven days of the workweek. The 7th day overtime law does not apply when the employee is not permitted to work over 40 hours total in the workweek. The state adopts the federal minimum wage rate by reference if the federal rate is greater than the State rate. Compensating time in lieu of overtime is allowed upon written request by an employee of any county, charter county, consolidated local government, or urban-county government, including an employee of a county-elected official.
