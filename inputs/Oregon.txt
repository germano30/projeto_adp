For the state of the Oregon the laws of mimum wage is: Basic Minimum Rate (per hour): $15.05 (standard state rate); $16.30 (Portland Metro Area); $14.05 (Non-Urban counties) Premium Pay After Designated Hours The overtime premium rate is one and one-half times the employee's regular rate, unless otherwise specified. : Weekly - 40 Premium pay required after 10 hours a day in nonfarm canneries, driers, or packing plants and in mills, factories or manufacturing establishments (excluding sawmills, planning mills, shingle mills, and logging camps). The Oregon minimum wage is adjusted annually on July 1 based on a set formula. There is a higher minimum wage in the Portland metro area and a lower minimum wage in non-urban counties.
